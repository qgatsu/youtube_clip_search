About Clip Finder

Paste the URL of an original YouTube stream and quickly find related clip videos. We never download or re-host video files; everything is watched on YouTube.

How it works

We take the original video URL or ID and query the YouTube Data API to search public video titles and descriptions. Videos that mention the original URL are shown as clip candidates.

Results can be sorted by views, publish date, or duration, and you can mark favorites to keep track of interesting clips.

Notes

Clip Finder uses the YouTube Data API Services and only accesses public data. We do not access your Google account information or private content.
